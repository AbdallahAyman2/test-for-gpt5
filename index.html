Quick Notes

  • Newton's 2nd Law: F = m a
  • Work-Energy: ΔK = W, E_total conserved
  • Momentum: p = m v, conserved in isolation
  • Torque: τ = r × F = r F sinθ
  • SHM: T = 2π sqrt(L/g) (pendulum small-angle)
  • RC charge: Vc(t) = V(1 - e^{-t/RC})
  • Wave speed: v = f λ

Challenges

  1. Space Glider: Finish level 2 using less than 40% fuel.
  2. Torque Tycoon: Balance a 10 kg mass at 0.5 m with two smaller masses.
  3. Mirror Madness: Hit two targets using only one mirror.
  4. Projectile: Match a range of 50 m at g = 9.81 with and without drag.
